main.lua
  Features
    Can enable or disable the trees transparency and shadow.
    Can enable or disable the Hero Hunter's M1 texture, making it look more clean and lookalike FFlags.
    Can enable or disable the Martial Artist's M1 effect and texture, making it look more clean and lookalike FFlags.
    Can enable or disable the Hero Hunter's ground crack when someone uses the awakening.
    Can enable or disable the Hero Hunter's Clone effect on the Flowing Water move.
    Can enable or disable the auto jump in roblox, due to players accidentally jumping on mobile.
    Can enable or disable the FPS and Ping GUI that shows the accurate FPS and real Ping that is shown in Shift + F3.
    Can enable or disable the dragging of the FPS and Ping GUI.
    Saves the settings and will load the previous settings om script launch.
